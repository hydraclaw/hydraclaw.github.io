About HydraClaw

The HydraClaw Strap affixes to most water bottle cages and is designed to secure water bottles
from rough roads, railroad crossings, and spontaneous bottle launches.
Velcro strap is adjustable and fits best with 24oz and 25oz insulated water bottles.
Light-weight and durable.
Proudly made in the USA with marine grade shock cord and velcro straps.
The HydraClaw is available as a pair (2 straps) or as a single strap to affix to your behind the seat water bottle cage(s).
While this fits many bottles and cages, we recommend the CamelBak 24oz bottle for optimal performance.
